About Epison

The Epidemiological Society of Nigeria was registered in 2010 and currently has over 200 members. It is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria (CAC/IT/No 41843) and enjoys a joint membership status with the International Epidemiological Association (IEA). The main goals of the society include: To facilitate communication and co-operation among multidisciplinary individuals engaged in practice, research and teaching of epidemiology throughout Nigeria...

MEMBERSHIP / Become a member

The Epidemiological Society of Nigeria (EPiSON) currently has membership strength of over 250, spread over all the states in Nigeria. The membership is from various disciplines such as medicine, veterinary medicine, pharmacy, laboratory science, statistics, sociology, parasitology, nursing just to mention a few. The Society has a multi-disciplinary team for any form of Epidemiological work. .
