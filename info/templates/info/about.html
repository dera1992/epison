ABOUT EPISON
Brief Description

The Epidemiological Society of Nigeria was registered in 2010 and currently has over 200 members. It is registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission of Nigeria (CAC/IT/No 41843) and enjoys a joint membership status with the International Epidemiological Association (IEA). The main goals of the society include:

To facilitate communication and co-operation among multidisciplinary individuals engaged in practice, research and teaching of epidemiology throughout Nigeria.

To engage in the development and use of epidemiological methods in all fields of health including social, community and preventive medicine

To collect and analyze information on effective epidemiological methods/ strategies and approaches and to promote such strategies through the exchange of information and ideas among the members.

To encourage volunteerism in support of epidemiology and provide the means by which it can be put into effect.

To engage in other activities that would promote the attainment of the aims and objectives set above.
